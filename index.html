About Me

I'm an Information Systems student at BYU who loves all things tech. Whether I'm building websites, messing around with new tools, or gaming with friends, I've always been drawn to creating things in the digital world.

I've had the chance to work on some cool projects, like implementing AI-powered automation at River Logistics and competing in the BYU Marriott Case Competition, where my team placed 2nd out of 98 with our Adminify AI solution. I'm all about working with great teams and bringing ideas to life. I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into clean, efficient solutions that actually make a difference.
